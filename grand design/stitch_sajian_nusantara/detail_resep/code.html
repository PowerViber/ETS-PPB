Rendang Daging Sapi Padang

A slow-cooked masterpiece of beef braised in coconut milk and a rich spice paste until the liquid evaporates and the meat turns dark, tender, and intensely flavorful.

The Base

The Spiced Paste (Bumbu)

01
The Infusion Begins

In a large heavy-bottomed pot or wok, combine the coconut milk, spice paste, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and turmeric leaf. Bring to a gentle boil, stirring continuously to prevent the coconut milk from breaking.

02
Slow Reduction

Add the beef cubes. Reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally. This is the patience phase—the liquid must slowly reduce and thicken into a "Kalio" state before turning into Rendang.
